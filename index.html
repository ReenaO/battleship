How to play:

Players:

2

Goals:

To sink other players ships

Setup:

Each players puts there ships on bottom grid

Ships

Each player has 5 ships which they can put on grid horizontally or vertically not diagonnally

Ships can't hang off grid

Ships

  • Carrier - 5 squares
  • Battleship - 4 squares
  • Cruiser - 3 squares
  • Submarine - 3 squares
  • Destroyer - 2 squares

Rules:

Take turns taking shots by typing grid coordinates

Oppenent must state hit or miss

In the top grid mark squares white is a miss -red is a hit for shots you fire

Must tell other player when they have sunk a ship

Winner:

First one to sink all oppenents ships wins
